BOSS KATANA 50 MK-2

This is a two-channel guitar combo amplifier, part of the second generation of the extremely popular Katana line. All devices in this series offer guitarists almost all the necessary types of sound and effects, as well as many useful functions, such as recording without using microphones and sound cards, the ability to play with headphones, as well as fine-tune the sound using related software.
